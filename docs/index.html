Enhance your app switching experience with a customizable, quick-launch overlay and app-specific hotkeys.

Download Latest Version

Requires macOS 14.0 or later

Why BetterTab?

Enhanced App Switcher

Activate a sleek overlay of running apps with a global hotkey (default: Option + Tab).

Quick Filtering

Instantly filter apps by typing their name. Supports prefix and fuzzy search for faster navigation.

App-Specific Hotkeys

Define custom keyboard shortcuts ("App Binds") to launch or switch to your most-used applications directly.

Customizable

Personalize appearance with themes for preferences, choose accent colors, and decide if BetterTab launches at login.
